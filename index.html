FAQ

What is the role of Nerko?

Nerko provides guidance on managing personal finances, investments, retirement planning, and wealth management. We help clients make informed decisions to achieve their financial goals.

What services does Nerko offer?

We provide a comprehensive range of financial and accounting services, including tax planning and preparation, auditing, bookkeeping, financial consulting, business advisory, and more.

What industries does Nerko specialize in?

We have expertise across various industries, including but not limited to healthcare, real estate, manufacturing, technology, and professional services. Our team's diverse skills allow us to cater to a wide range of business sectors.

How can Nerko assist small businesses?

We offer tailored solutions for small businesses, including financial analysis, budgeting, payroll services, and assistance with compliance and regulatory requirements. Our goal is to support small businesses in achieving financial success.

What types of financial software does Nerko use?

We utilize industry-leading financial software to streamline processes and enhance accuracy. Our software includes accounting platforms, tax preparation software, and data analytics tools.
